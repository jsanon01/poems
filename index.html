MY POEMS

If You Can Challenge!

“Time and tide never wait!”
Stop them if you can!


Fog and wind often in action!
Catch them if you can!


Involuntary done first time,
Errors inherited from humans
Who will fix them if they can.


Guys, are people powerless
Over physiological needs? Yes.
They’ll ban them if they can.


As mansions and millions interact,
When Death is at their doors
Well-offs will stop Death if they can.


As lies spread million times,
Truth can stop them in a second.
“Sun can’t be covered with fingers.”


God freely gives
Evil mercilessly takes
“Blessed, don’t be depressed.”

Life Is A Puzzle!

Well, life is a puzzle
Mankind will make it,
Once, all pieces F.I.T.


Regardless of sinners,
Along with story tellers
They can’t live without F.I.T.


Food is there to sustain!
Plenty of food for all,
Thus, wrong repartition.


Info is there to remain!
From day one to end,
Nothing changes in it.


Tool frees us from pain!
More the tool is worthy
More mankind needs it.


No one can escape their destiny!
Unless they find the puzzle,
A sine qua non condition.


Poverty is human made!
Info and Tool are God Made!
Have we puzzled them yet?


Well, ladies and gentlemen,
Lack of food, knowledge and skills
Separate mankind mercilessly.

No Beauty is Permanent!

“In the eyes of the beholder,”
Beauty is as enjoyable
As pictures are memorable.


As an advertising tool,
Beauty is money-oriented,
Worth having, and watching.


As wrinkles get bold,
“They are skin folds”
And there to stay.


As people are getting old
To their bones, they are cold.
No condition is permanent.


Humans and Mother Nature
Go toward one direction:
“Born, live, and die.”


Sunshine is to youngster
What sunset is to elderly.
No beauty is permanent.
